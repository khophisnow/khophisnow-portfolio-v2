# Kiop Samba lesson

Kiop was a reminder that old exposed services can carry serious risk. The challenge focused on service discovery, vulnerable Samba review, and the impact of unpatched legacy software.

## What I practiced

- Initial service enumeration.
- Confirming risky legacy services.
- Understanding why public exploits work against outdated systems.
- Turning the lesson back into defensive thinking.

## What it taught me

Patch management is security work. If a service is exposed, old, and reachable, it becomes part of the attack surface whether the main app is well built or not.
